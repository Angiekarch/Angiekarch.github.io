Who We Are

We are a small pop-up cafe business. About once per season we set up a little cafe in an interesting location for a week. We focus on creating pleasant atmospheres in unique places. To help foster creative conversation, all of our tables are covered in paper and we make custom scented crayons for people to draw with as they talk and drink their coffee.

Coffee

Indulge in the rich and invigorating symphony of flavors with our meticulously sourced and expertly roasted coffee beans, delivering a perfect balance of boldness and smoothness in every sip, elevating your coffee experience to new heights.

Crayons

Explore a world of creativity with our scented crayons, where vivid colors and delightful aromas converge, transforming each stroke into a sensory masterpiece that sparks imagination and delight.
